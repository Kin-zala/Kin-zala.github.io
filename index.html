Bioinformatics Tools

GeneScope is an interactive Streamlit application designed to enable non-bioinformatic researchers to explore gene expression data without writing code. Users can upload CSV datasets and generate boxplots, heatmaps, and PCA visualizations with integrated KMeans clustering. The app includes log2 transformation options, top variable gene selection, and fully interactive Plotly charts. All plots and clustering results can be downloaded as PNG and CSV files for further analysis or reporting.
